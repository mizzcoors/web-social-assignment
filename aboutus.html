About Us

The History

Turmec Teoranta has been based in the Gaeltacht at Rathcairn, Co Meath since 1972, with over 45 years of experience in the engineering and recycling industries. Our experience is dedicated to finding the best solutions for your requirements, giving you peace of mind with tried and tested approaches fused with design expertise to address your specific requirements. Our aim is to provide the most cost-effective and high-quality solutions possible. As part of our renowned complete aftersales support we provide full training to you and your staff to ensure the smooth running of any installations, plant or solutions we provide

Our Future

Turmec has grown from strength to strength over the years, We pride ourselves on creating partnerships with our clients and see ourselves as a key part in the ongoing development and ultimate success of every client we work with. We aim to produce a truly collaborative design for every project we undertake, that not only addresses current challenges but futureproofs the plant for further business growth.
Whether you need a completely new plant or a bespoke solution to an existing problem, we bring years of unique industry experience and proven design solutions to every problem.
