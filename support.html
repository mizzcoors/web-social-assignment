Support

Support through the years

Through out the years Turmec prides it's self in the support and close relationships we form with our customers, we ensure that within a 24hr window, we are onsite to sort any problem big or small

Onsite services

We pride ourselves on our close aftersales support of our clients in both service and maintenance and believe our success is built on how we partner with our clients throughout the process.
We firmly believe your success is our success.
We are able to respond quickly to incidental breakdowns and also provide both preventative and scheduled maintenance of your installation. This can include registration of measurements, inspections and equipment condition reports. We can provide Service Level Agreements to any required level of support and response time required and have many years experience in supporting our international installations.

Spare Parts

We are able to provide spare parts for any section of your installation that may require it, and also carry a complete range of spare parts for all recycling plants and most screening plants.
We know that downtime costs your business money, so we aim to facilitate a repair within the shortest possible amount of time – usually within 24 hours. Spare parts are available through our depot network in the UK and Ireland. At your request, we can advise on a partial or complete modernisation of your plant or installation. Contact us and we will source everything you require in the shortest time possible..

Training

Turmec provides a training programme approved by the client for supervisory, operation and maintenance personnel.We provide training to the client’s operations staff in all aspects of the works to ensure the safe and effective use of the plant and machinery.Our mentors that are responsible for training, are highly experienced and have a thorough understanding of the equipment and the process.Training include hands on operation of the plant and its machinery, as well as classroom tuition. We ensure that all relevant clients staff are competent at controlling the plant and maintaining equipment including electrical controls, computer and operating systems.
